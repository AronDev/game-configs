#### Blink
> #showtooltip  
> /stopcasting  
> /cast Blink  

* This macro will cancel your current spell cast and make you blink away.

---

#### Counterspell on focus
> #showtooltip  
> /stopcasting  
> /cast `[target=focus]`Counterspell  

* This macro will cast Counterspell on your focus target.
